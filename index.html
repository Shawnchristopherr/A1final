My Ideal Job: Lead Cyber Security Analyst

This position is that of a Lead Cyber Security Analyst.
A lead cyber security analyst is an individual tasked with leading a team responsible for the client’s IT infrastructure. This includes round-the-clock monitoring, detection, triaging and analysis of security alerts, detection and prevention of intrusions, vulnerability testing with reporting on appropriate mitigations and so on.
There are a few reasons why this position is appealing to me.
Firstly, this is a job that is in high demand and will continue to become even more so as the world continually shifts to going completely online, and with it, more cybercriminals. As more and more data joins the internet the more the security of it becomes vital in order to protect people from those that would cause harm.
Secondly, it’s fun, interesting and rewarding work.
You will never feel like you’re doing archaic, unnecessary work. In a world where threats and technology are ever-evolving, so too does its defence need to be.
There is great satisfaction in doing work that requires you to be on your toes, keeping your mind constantly engaged, and even better that doing so directly helps protects peoples livelihoods.

Skills, qualifications and experience required for this position:

Strong technical predilection, knowledge and propensity for continuous autonomous learning and development in security technology controls, proxy, IDS/IPS, DDOS, SIEM, Cyber Threat Intelligence, Threat hunting and incident response.
Leadership skills and experience, with emphasis on the ability to train, mentor and develop a high-performance technical team.
Information Technology degree or similar with Information Security industry certifications.

Skills, qualifications and experience I currently possess:

Above-average technical aptitude and knowledge.
Willingness and motivation to learn about all things relevant to the job
Leadership skills
Information Technology degree currently underway
